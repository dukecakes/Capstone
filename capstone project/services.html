Choosing who will care for your child is an extremely important decision. Finding an environment where you trust that your child will be safe, and well-nurtured will help relieve some of the stress that often comes with making this difficult decision.

Here are the steps for getting started:-

  • Review the Day Care Program Basics which outline the program process.
  • Attend an upcoming Parent Information Session face to face, using mobile, or join us on a webinar.
  • Complete the Child Care Application.
  • Find a qualified child care provider.
  • Interview our providers.
  • Become Part of Our Family.

Become A Part of Our organization

Our Day Care has been a trusted child care and education facility for families in the Small Heath area since 1976. Since we started, we have worked hard to connect families with the best child care educators. Our program provides care and early education to children in a convenient setting starting at 10 weeks of age.
