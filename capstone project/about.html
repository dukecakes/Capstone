Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

Our Day Care is a network of professional child care educators who all believe that learning begins at birth. We support the emotional, social and mental development of toddlers, preschoolers and middle-age children in safe and polished environments.

Goals

  • We will provide the best quality development opportunities that support our child care workforce to obtain a degree in early childhood education or a closely related field.
  • We will regularly monitor the quality of the development experiences for our child care educators through surveys, interviews, input from parents, and conferences.
  • We will cultivate joint partnerships with local, and national organizations which are also interested in elevating the importance and easy accessibility of quality child care.

Become A Part of Our organization

Our Day Care has been a trusted child care and education facility for families in the Small Heath area since 1976. Since we started, we have worked hard to connect families with the best child care educators. Our program provides care and early education to children in a convenient setting starting at 10 weeks of age.
