Plan

Our organization has been a trusted child care Service in the entire Small Heath area since 1976. We are the only licensed Day Care System in Small Heath. When you join our Day Care family, we refer you to child care educators with approved licenses. We do more than match your family with a child care educator. We stay in touch with every educator by home visits, training and mentoring them, and by providing them with better career opportunities. Our child care educators provide personal care to each child in a safe, and creative environment.

We Provide Child Care Guidance Services

We partner with other Child Care organizations of Small Heath to provide training and technical assistance to care providers. Technical assistance to providers includes information and materials about child development, nutrition, quality enhancement and other child care service issues.

Development Opportunities

We help people who want to start up their own child care business, approved under our license, by providing guidance to them. Become A Part of Our organization
